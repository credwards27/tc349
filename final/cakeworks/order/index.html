How to Order

Pick a Shape

Choose from a variety of base cake shapes.

Pick a Size

Specify the dimensions of the cake, or enter the number of people it needs to serve.

Choose How Many Layers

You can stack up to four layers in one cake. If you want more than one layer, be sure to choose your filling flavor too!

Choose your Flavors

Mix and match flavors by layer.

Choose a Base Color

What color of frosting would you like covering the whole cake?

Write your Own Text

Enter in exactly what you want your cake to say. Don't forget to pick a color too!

Upload an image

Upload an image if you want one printed on your cake. Don't forget to tell us where to put it on the cake in the Comments box.

When do you need it?

Tell us the date of your event, and we can have it ready for pickup, or delivered and set up for you!

Don't Forget Details

Use the Comments box to give us any other details about your order (special coloring, text arrangement, image placement, special designs, etc.)
