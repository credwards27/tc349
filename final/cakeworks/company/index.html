CakeWorks is a local cake bakery that specializes in all kinds of custom-made cakes for many different events. From birthdays and weddings to graduations and retirements, we do it all. Personalized service is our specialty, and we aim to give you the highest quality cakes at incredibly low prices.

Whatever the occasion, CakeWorks is committed to providing an excellent experience from start to finish, even beyond the cake itself. We give you one-on-one service to ensure you get the cake you really want, flexible pricing options so you don't break the bank, and even offer pickup or delivery on your time. You have plenty of things to worry about when planning an event, and stressing over the cake shouldn't be one of them.

History

CakeWorks started with just a girl doing what she loved. Not too many years later it has become a successful family business serving up smiles far and wide!

CakeWorks first opened to the public in 1994 with a wedding cake delivered to Farmington, Michigan.
